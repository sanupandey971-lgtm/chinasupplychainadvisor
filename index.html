How Our Investigation Process Works

Step 1 – Client Request
Clients provide supplier information such as company name, website, or factory location.

Step 2 – Background Investigation
We analyze company registration data, ownership structure, legal disputes, and reputation.

Step 3 – Field Verification (If Required)

Step 4 – Risk Assessment Report
Clients receive a structured investigation report outlining risks and recommendations.
