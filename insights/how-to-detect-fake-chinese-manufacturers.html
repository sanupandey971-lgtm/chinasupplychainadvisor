How To Detect Fake Chinese Manufacturers

Many overseas buyers lose money because they trust online supplier profiles without independent verification. Fake manufacturers usually operate by pretending to own production facilities while actually acting as trading intermediaries.

Common Signs of Fake Manufacturers

  • No verifiable factory address
  • Unrealistic production capacity claims
  • Lack of equipment or workforce evidence
  • Reluctance to conduct video verification
  • Prices significantly lower than market average

Verification Methods

  • Check business registration records
  • Request production facility photos
  • Analyze export history
  • Conduct independent investigation
