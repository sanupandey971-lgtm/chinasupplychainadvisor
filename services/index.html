Our 25-Point Supplier Due Diligence Framework

Our investigations follow a structured framework designed to penetrate factory marketing claims and verify the real operational and financial capabilities of suppliers.

Legal & Financial

Business license verification, paid-in capital, legal representative background and ownership structure.

Production Reality

Staff verification, production line inspection, subcontracting analysis and equipment evaluation.

Quality Control

Incoming inspection systems, IPQC checkpoints, final QC reporting and defect handling processes.

Compliance

ISO/BSCI verification, environmental compliance, IP risk and workplace safety standards.

Delivery Reliability

Lead-time analysis, client reference checks, packaging standards and after-sales policies.
