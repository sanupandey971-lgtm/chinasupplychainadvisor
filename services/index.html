Independent Supplier Audit & Factory Verification

Penetrating the marketing façade of factories and verifying their real operational capability.

1. Legal & Financial Verification

  • Business License Verification – confirm registration data with official government records.
  • Real Paid-in Capital – evaluate actual capital contribution and financial stability.
  • Legal Representative Background – check litigation history and credit restrictions.
  • Ownership Structure – identify hidden shareholders or intermediary shell companies.
  • Export License Verification – confirm whether the factory has direct export rights.
